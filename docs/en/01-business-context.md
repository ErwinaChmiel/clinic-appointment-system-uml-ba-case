# Business context

## Background

MediVisit is an online appointment booking system for a medium-sized medical clinic. The current process relies mostly on phone-based bookings and manual schedule management, which causes errors, reception overload and limited visibility of available slots for patients.

## Business problem

The clinic wants to reduce phone calls, shorten the booking process and improve control over doctor availability. An additional goal is to support prepayments for selected services and automate appointment confirmations.

## Goals

| ID | Business goal | Success metric |
|---|---|---|
| BG-01 | Reduce phone-based bookings | At least 40% of appointments booked online |
| BG-02 | Reduce scheduling errors | Less than 2% monthly slot conflicts |
| BG-03 | Automate confirmations | 95% of confirmations sent automatically |
| BG-04 | Support prepayments | Prepayment configurable per service |
