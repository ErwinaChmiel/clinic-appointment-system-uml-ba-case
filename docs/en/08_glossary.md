# Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Patient | Person booking a medical appointment. |
| Specialist | Doctor or medical service provider. |
| Slot | Specific time interval available for an appointment. |
| Temporary reservation | Short-term slot lock during the booking process. |
| Prepayment | Payment required before appointment confirmation. |
| Confirmed appointment | Appointment that meets booking conditions and is visible in the schedule. |
