# Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Patient | A person booking a medical appointment. |
| Doctor | A person providing a medical service. |
| Appointment slot | A specific time interval available for an appointment. |
| Temporary reservation | A short-term slot hold created during the booking process and kept until payment is completed or the payment session expires. |
| Prepayment | An online payment required before appointment confirmation. |
| Payment | The process of authorizing an online prepayment through an external payment gateway. |
| Failed payment | A payment that was declined, interrupted or not completed within the required time. |
| Confirmed appointment | An appointment confirmed after successful payment authorization and visible in the schedule. |
| Notification | An e-mail or SMS message sent to the patient in relation to booking, changing or cancelling an appointment. |
| Schedule | A set of available appointment slots for a doctor, managed by reception staff or an administrator. |
| Appointment status | The current state of an appointment, for example pending payment, confirmed, payment failed, cancelled, no-show, in progress or completed. |
| Payment gateway | An external system responsible for online payment authorization. |
| Notification system | A component or service responsible for sending e-mail or SMS messages to patients. |
