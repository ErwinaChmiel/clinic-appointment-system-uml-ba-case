# Scope and stakeholders

## Solution scope

The system covers available slot search, appointment booking, optional prepayment, appointment cancellation, doctor availability management and notification delivery.

## Out of scope

- patient medical records,
- teleconsultations,
- prescriptions,
- integration with public healthcare systems,
- advanced financial analytics.

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibility | Need |
|---|---|---|
| Patient | Books and cancels appointments | Fast access to available slots |
| Receptionist | Handles exceptions and schedules | Less manual work |
| Doctor | Provides availability | Up-to-date schedule |
| Administrator | Configures the system | Control over users and services |
| Clinic management | Evaluates process efficiency | Measurable process improvement |
